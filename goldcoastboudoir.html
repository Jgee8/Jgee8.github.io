Home Web Design

Gold Coast Boudoir

Challenge:

Gold Coast Boudoir is a photography studio based in Orlando and Chicago. They needed a homepage redesign to meet Google Ads content policies and improve overall clarity, while expanding on their bold, editorial style.

Result:

I redesigned the homepage with clearer navigation, a stronger visual hierarchy, and calls to action that guided users more effectively. Layout and imagery were updated to meet ad policy guidelines while building on the brand's original aesthetic. The new design lifted ad restrictions and contributed to a measurable increase in conversions for their new ad build.
