Home Web Design

Stephanie Marie

Challenge:

Stephanie Marie was a current boudoir photographer client expanding into business and branding photography. She wanted a polished, professional website based on a specific visual style she provided as a reference. However, her existing site was built using a deprecated Flothemes builder, which significantly limited layout flexibility and design customization.

Result:

I implemented a custom redesign within the legacy Flothemes platform, using code to work around its limitations and expand on her clear visual direction. The new site emphasized clarity, professionalism, and visual consistency, while also applying best practices in accessibility and performance, including structured content, image optimization, and descriptive alt text. The final result aligned with her evolving brand and set the foundation for her growth into new client segments.
